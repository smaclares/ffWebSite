About Us

We are a local, South Floridian pet grooming store that has been in business since 1991. Billy, our go-to-guy, has been grooming for over 30 years! Our staff is well-trained in providing the best grooming and pet care for your fuzzy companion, no matter how large or small.

We also stock specialty pet items! Have a question? We're happy to answer any question about your pet needs.
